# 📊 Sales Uplift Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview

The **Sales Uplift Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence dashboard designed to monitor and analyze sales performance. It provides a comprehensive overview of key business metrics, enabling organizations to make data-driven decisions through intuitive visualizations and filters.

The dashboard presents insights into total sales, customer transactions, regional performance, product sales, category-wise revenue, monthly trends, and sales channel distribution. Users can apply filters such as **Region**, **Month**, and **Sales Channel** to explore the data from different perspectives.

---

## ✨ Dashboard Features

* 📈 Displays overall business performance using Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
* 🌍 Analyzes sales across different regions.
* 📅 Tracks monthly sales trends.
* 🛍️ Identifies top-selling products.
* 📦 Compares sales across product categories.
* 🌐 Compares Online and Offline sales channels.
* 🔍 Interactive filters for Region, Month, and Sales Channel.
